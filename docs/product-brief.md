# Product Brief: Inside Pitch

## One-line position

An office IPL prediction game that transforms every matchday into a fast ritual, a team rivalry, and a season-long leaderboard battle.

## Core behavior design

- Short-term dopamine: daily winner picks, confidence calls, and instant leaderboard movement
- Mid-term motivation: weekly winner celebrations and streak protection
- Long-term ambition: season champion, team champion, and second-chance redemption

## MVP scope

- Daily match list with a locked prediction cutoff before toss
- Winner pick, confidence multiplier, and optional margin bonus
- Global, weekly, and team leaderboards
- Streak tracking
- Manual admin control for match setup and result entry

## Why this should be public, not anonymous

This product relies on social energy more than pure gameplay depth. Public scores create banter, team identity, and visible movement, which are the strongest retention drivers in an office setting.

## Reward strategy

- Default reward: bragging rights, Slack spotlight, and weekly winner shoutouts
- Light real reward: small gift cards only for playoff and final winners

This avoids over-optimizing for prize hunting while keeping the top end of the season meaningful.

## Tie-breakers

1. Prediction hit rate
2. Successful underdog picks
3. Earliest locked prediction

This sequence keeps the system fair, easy to explain, and hard to game.

## Retention safeguards

- Second Chance League halfway through the season
- Weekly winners and specialty awards
- Multiple leaderboard angles so more users stay competitive
- Scarce power-ups distributed over the full season

## Operational note

Even if a cricket data API is used, the product should always ship with an admin fallback for match creation, result correction, and score recalculation.
